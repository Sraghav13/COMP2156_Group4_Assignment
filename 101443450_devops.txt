The COMP2156 DevOps course addresses a broad spectrum of subjects, such as continuous integration, continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, containerization, configuration management, monitoring, and collaboration tools.
Students acquire in-demand skills valued by employers in software development and IT operations, empowering them to proficiently oversee and automate the software development process.
Leveraging DevOps practices effectively ensures a streamlined process for deploying code to production servers, implementing hot-fixes, identifying errors preemptively, and maintaining transparency and communication among all teams. 
Viewing the software development lifecycle from the perspective of a DevOps Engineer enhances developers' knowledge and expertise.
