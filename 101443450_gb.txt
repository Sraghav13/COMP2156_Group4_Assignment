George Brown College (GBC) is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It was Established in 1967, it is one of the top colleges in Canada known for its innovative programs, industry connections, and diverse student population.
It has 3 campuses - Casa Loma , Waterfront , and St.James .
All of the 3 campuses of george brown college are located in the downtown, heart of toronto .
George Brown College draws students from diverse backgrounds worldwide due to its recognized commitment to high-quality education and its emphasis on embracing global viewpoints.
The college fosters innovation and entrepreneurial spirit through initiatives such as the George Brown College Centre for Business and the Digital Media and Gaming Incubator.
George Brown College has partnerships with various industry leaders, providing students with opportunities for internships, co-op placements, and applied research projects.
