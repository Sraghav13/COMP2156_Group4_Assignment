In this course we will gain knowledge of the protocols, methods, and best practices that a DevOps engineer uses in the workplace to boost output in this course. Utilize an industry-standard toolbox to conduct practical work while analyzing and assessing the many developer operation implementations that are accessible. Develop a solution for a challenging business issue while upholding the fundamental duties of DevOps.Along with DevOps tools like Ansible and Git, students will learn about data formats and types such as JavaScript Object Notation (JSON), YAML (Yaml Ain't Markup Language), YANG (Yet Another Next Generation), and their use in network automation.
